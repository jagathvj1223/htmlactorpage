vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema. He was born on June 22, 1974 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. His father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, is a film director and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer and vocalist. Vijay made his cinematic debut in 1984 with Vetri, directed by his father. He made his debut as a lead actor with Naalaiya Theerpu (1992) at the age of 18. He has been credited in more than 60 movies, including Leo, Varisu, and Greatest of All Time. His songs include Yaar Indha Saalai Oram, Nenjam Oru Murai, and Kelamal Kaiyile.
